Research

Meeting with Expert: Mr. Scott Sarnikowski, Founder and CEO of Wild Plum Software

Meeting Date: Oct 31, 2014

Attendees: Brick Masters and Mr. Sarnikowski

Mr. S has made a German Language learning app called VerbenTanz. It is a dynamic learning tool focused on learning German verbs. It helps students learn verbs with increasing levels of complexity. The app has nine different levels and two styles of taking quizzes. The app is designed to promote rapid learning. The quizzes are uniquely designed to test for both speed and accuracy.

Mr. S also used brain study and the learning pyramid just like we did. He said that we were correct on how use of association and stimulating multiple parts of the brain does help with retention. In fact, he said he had done research about these things when he was studying developmental psychology at the University of Minnesota!

He explained to us that the way older kids and adults learn a language is very different from the way babies learn. In fact it has been shown that when babies learn a language they use a completely different part of the brain than when adults learn. When adults learn they use the voluntary function of the brain, whereas babies learn via the involuntary function of the brain.

When you have mastered a language, you are able to instantly respond to sentences in that language. He has used this principle in developing quizzes to test retention. Quizzes in the app test not only whether you got a correct answer but also how quickly you were able to respond.

We really enjoyed our session with Mr. S. He was very friendly, he showed us how his app works, gave us a free copy of the app, and offered to meet with us again!
