About Brail

BRAIL stands for Better Retention and Individualized Learning
This project was started as part of the First Lego League Competition of 2015
Our team is called the BrickMasters!!!
Our team members are Arnav Bhola, Arya Vasani, Arnav Shah, Rishi Atreya, Tej Bade, Rohan Chakravarthi, Adithya Peruvemba
We represent the Argonaut Elementary School, Saratoga, CA

Our Project

Is about improving how kids learn and retain information through individualized learning methods. Every kid learns differently.
Our goal is to create learning tracks with different methods of learning like music, games, activities, audio/visual, and practice.

Start by taking the assessment test which will help you learn the way you like or help you choose the learning track.
